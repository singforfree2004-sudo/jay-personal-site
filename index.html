我是徐志杰（Jay Hsu），現任南臺科技大學流行音樂產業系副教授。我的工作大多發生在教室、社區、活動現場與研究之間：帶學生做企劃、陪地方做文化行動，也把障礙研究與共融觀點放進專業課程。AI 對我來說是一個好用的工作方法，但不是主角；真正重要的是，人怎麼透過教育、創作與合作，讓社會變得更容易互相理解。I’m Jay Hsu, Associate Professor at the Department of Popular Music Industry, Southern Taiwan University of Science and Technology. My work moves between classrooms, communities, cultural projects, and research. I teach students to plan, create, and collaborate in real contexts, while bringing disability studies and inclusion into professional education. AI is useful to me as a working method, but the larger question is how education, creativity, and collaboration can help people understand one another better.

AI 教學與專案工作流AI in Teaching & Project Workflows

AI 不是我網站的主角，但它確實改變了我做教學、行政、企劃與研究整理的方式。我感興趣的是：如何讓 AI 成為可靠的工作方法，而不是一次性的工具展示。AI is not the main theme of this website, but it has changed how I teach, plan, organize research, and manage project documentation. I am interested in how AI can become a reliable working method rather than a one-time tool demonstration.

01

先從課程與任務開始Start with teaching and tasks

先看老師、學生、助理與專案團隊真正卡住的地方：作業、提案、報告、活動紀錄、成果整理與溝通。I start by identifying where teachers, students, assistants, and project teams actually get stuck: assignments, proposals, reports, event records, outcome documentation, and communication.

02

把經驗變成可傳承的方法Turn experience into shareable methods

真正有用的不是「某個人很會用工具」，而是把做事的方法留下來，讓學生、助理與合作夥伴都能接得上。The goal is not to have one tool expert. The goal is to leave behind working methods that students, assistants, and partners can understand and continue using.

03

保留人的判斷與風格Keep human judgment and voice

AI 對我來說最有價值的地方，是減少重複勞動，讓人有更多力氣回到關係、創意、判斷與社會實踐。For me, AI is most valuable when it reduces repetitive labor and gives people more energy for relationships, creativity, judgment, and social practice.
